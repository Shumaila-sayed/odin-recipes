Miso Soup

Description

Did you know you can make restaurant-worthy miso soup in the comfort of your own home? Well, you can! You just need a good recipe â€” and that's where we come in. This miso soup recipe is bold, flavorful, and easy to make with just five ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste. Stir in tofu.
  3. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup.
  4. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
